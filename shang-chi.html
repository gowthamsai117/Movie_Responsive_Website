MOVIE NAME : SHANG CHI


ACTOR NAME : SIMU LIU
ACTRESS NAME : AWKWAFINA
DIRECTOR : DESTIN DANIEL CRETTON

Shang-Chi begins training in martial arts and is sent by Wenwu at the age of 14 to assassinate the leader of the Iron Gang. After fulfilling his mission, a traumatized Shang-Chi escapes to San Francisco, where he adopts the name "Shaun" . In the present day, Shang-Chi works as a valet with his best friend Katy.
